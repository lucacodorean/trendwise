# Context

## Glossary

### Stock

A publicly traded equity instrument selected by ticker symbol, with company name and exchange used to disambiguate when available.

### Stock Comparison

An analysis view containing one primary Stock and up to two comparison Stocks for the same Forecast Horizon. Each selected Stock has its own Stock Prediction, and each Forecast Graph may be shown or hidden independently.

### Stock Summary

A concise explanation of a selected Stock's current context, including recent performance, relevant company news, External Factors, and interpretation of its Stock Forecast or Stock Prediction.

### Key Factor

A concise explanation of a source signal or condition that helps interpret a Stock Prediction, grounded in Market Snapshots, Company News, External Factors, or Stock Forecast data.

### Stock Prediction

A discrete analytical signal about the expected market direction for a stock over a defined horizon, summarized with confidence, expected change, and risk level. A Stock Prediction may be derived from a Stock Forecast or generated independently from other signals. A Stock Prediction is not a trading recommendation.

### Stock Forecast

A projected future price behavior for a stock over a defined horizon, often expressed as an expected price, price range, or confidence interval. A Stock Forecast is grounded in observed market data and External Factors, not prior Stock Predictions.

### Market Snapshot

Observed real-world market data captured from a market data provider at a specific time. Market Snapshots are distinct from computed Stock Forecasts.

### Forecast Horizon

The future time window selected for a Stock Forecast or Stock Prediction. Canonical Forecast Horizons are `30m`, `1d`, `5d`, `7d`, `1mo`, `6mo`, and `1y`.

### External Factor

Non-price-market information that may influence a stock's future behavior, such as company news, earnings events, macroeconomic indicators, sector movement, market sentiment, analyst ratings, and major geopolitical or regulatory events.

### Forecast Graph

A visual representation of historical price context and projected future price behavior for a selected Forecast Horizon. Historical and forecasted values must be clearly distinguishable.

### Line Forecast

A Forecast Graph that represents price behavior as a continuous expected price path.

### Candlestick Forecast

A Forecast Graph that represents price behavior using estimated open, high, low, and close values for each forecast interval.

### Risk Level

An assessment of the uncertainty and volatility around a Stock Prediction. Risk Level does not represent personalized investment advice.
